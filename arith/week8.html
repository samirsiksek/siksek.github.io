Programme for Active Period in Arithmetic Geometry
Week 8: Monday 2 – Friday 6 June

  • Wednesday 4 June, Seminar
    Time and Room: 11-12, B3.02
    Speaker: Jan Tuitman (Leuven)
    Title: p-adic cohomology and counting points
    abstract : We will talk about p-adic methods for computing the Hasse-Weil zeta function of a curve or a surface over a finite field. After introducing some p-adic cohomology we will focus on families of curves in toric surfaces and explain how one can apply deformation and fibration techniques to them to obtain fast point counting algorithms.
