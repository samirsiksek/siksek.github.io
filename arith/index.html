Events

  • Monday 14–Friday 18 April 2008 Surfaces: Geometry and Arithmetic (Instructional Workshop)
  • Short courses (see below) and a regular number theory seminar
  • 16–20 June 2008 Rational Points on Curves and Higher-Dimensional Varieties



Short Courses

In addition to the week-long workshops above, we are planning a series of short courses (3–4 lectures, plus a problem session where appropriate), suitable for a broad number theory audience. These include:

  • Sir Peter Swinnerton-Dyer, The Manin conjecture
  • Tom Fisher, Visibility of Tate-Shafarevich Groups
  • Tim Dokchitser, The conjectures of Birch and Swinnerton-Dyer
  • Johan Bosman Galois representations of modular forms
  • William Hart, Introduction to Theta functions
  • Ambrus Pal, The Gross-Zagier formula
