Explicit Methods for Modular Curves
Taught Course Centre (TCC)

Times: Monday 13:00-15:00, for 8 weeks, starting 20 January

Topics include:

  • Galois representations of elliptic curves
  • Classification of subgroups of GL_2(F_p)
  • Modular curves and level structures, X(1), X_0(N), X_1(N), X_H
  • Arithmetic of curves, Jacobians, Mordell-Weil groups
  • The Eichler-Shimura theorem
  • Equations for modular curves
  • Sketch of Mazur's theorem on torsion of elliptic curves
